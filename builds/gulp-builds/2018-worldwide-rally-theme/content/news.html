• RALLY SIDE EVENTS AND MEETINGS

    by: Crystal Batayeh

    Interested in meeting with your team or fellow franchisees at Rally? Recommended times for side meetings include:

    •   • Monday 11 a.m. – 6 p.m.
    •   • Tuesday (lunch break) 12 – 2 p.m.
    •   • Wednesday after 7 p.m.
    •   • Thursday Noon – 6 p.m.

    You can book a conference room or ballroom for larger events (food, beverage and AV fees may apply). Smaller groups or social events have many Mandalay Bay restaurant choices. Please fill out this form for conference/ballrooms, or contact Jovana Dakic at mbhgroupdining@mandalaybay.com for restaurant reservations.
